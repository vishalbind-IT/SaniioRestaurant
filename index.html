Our History

A Tradition of Taste

Established in 2010, Saniio started as a small family bistro. Over the years, our passion for culinary excellence has transformed us into one of the city's premier dining destinations. We believe in the power of food to bring people together.

Our Head Chef, Marco Rossi, brings 20 years of international experience to the kitchen, fusing traditional techniques with modern flair. Every dish tells a story of heritage, flavor, and innovation.
